    Welcome to "Solitaire"! The goal of the game is to move the four aces, as they appear, to the foundations, and build each up in suit from ace to king. Turn cards face-up from the stock onto a pile. The top card of the pile may be played onto the foundations. Cards within the rows may be build down in sequence and alternating color. A sequence of cards may be moved as a unit from one pile to another.



    * reset - starts a new game

    * hint - gives you a hint if it can

    * help - shows this help

    * use buttons to select a card to move
